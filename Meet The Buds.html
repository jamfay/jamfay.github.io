About Island Buds

Who Are We?

We are a team of enterprising pharmacists advocating for the responsible use of medical marijuana. We produce home grown cannabis for the treatment of various pains and ailments.

  • Wayne Elliot - Founder and CEO of Island Buds Pharmaceuticals. His passion for botany has led him to developing a cannabis farm which is used to manufacture all of our goods.
  • Judith Sarjeant - Distribution Liason Officer. She partners with various pharmacies and doctors' offices to have our products sold.
  • Jamila Eastmond - Botanical scientist and lead cultivator. Her expertise ensures that we harvest only the highest grade of ganja for our valued customer base.
  • Shaniya Dennis - The company's marketing agent. She's in charge of advertising the company and our goods.
  • Carseen Greenidge - Our resident brand developer. Carseen is responsible for the product branding and the presentation of what you see on our shelves.
